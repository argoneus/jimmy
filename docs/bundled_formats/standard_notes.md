## Import to Joplin

Example: `jimmy-cli-linux "Standard Notes Backup - Sun Apr 28 2024 12_56_55 GMT+0200.zip" --format standard_notes`

## Known Limitations

Note links, attachments and folders are not implemented yet, since they require a subscription.
